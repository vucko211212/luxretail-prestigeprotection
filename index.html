Common Questions

Luxury Retail Security FAQ

Do you provide armed (Class G) officers for high-value inventory?

We offer both armed (Class G licensed) and unarmed officers. The right choice depends on your inventory, location, and environment, and we'll advise based on an assessment of your store.

Can you provide a Certificate of Insurance?

Yes. A Certificate of Insurance is available on request for our retail engagements, naming your store or property as required.

Do officers work plainclothes or uniformed?

Both. We match the presence to your brand and environment, from visible uniformed deterrence to discreet plainclothes loss prevention on the floor.

Can you integrate with our existing loss-prevention team?

Yes. Our officers coordinate with in-house loss prevention, follow your protocols, and provide written reporting that fits your existing process.

Do you cover high-value transport and open/close escort?

Secure opening and closing procedures and protected escort of high-value items are available where required. We build these into the coverage plan after a site assessment.

Are your officers trained for luxury retail environments specifically?

Yes. Beyond Florida licensing, our officers are trained in loss prevention, organized-retail-crime awareness, de-escalation, and hospitality-grade guest interaction, so security never disrupts the client experience.
